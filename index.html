TV Activation Instructions

Get your TV up and running in minutes

Activation Process

  1. Turn on your TV and ensure it's connected to Wi-Fi.
  2. Open the activation screen. It will display a unique activation code.
  3. On your smartphone or computer, go to the activation website.
  4. Enter the activation code shown on your TV screen.
  5. Sign in or create an account to complete the activation.
  6. Wait a few moments while the activation is processed.
  7. Once activated, your TV will refresh and display the home screen.
  8. If prompted, follow on-screen setup steps like choosing preferences or syncing accounts.
  9. Test by opening any streaming app to confirm successful activation.

Troubleshooting

  • No Internet? Ensure your router is working and try restarting it.
  • Invalid Code? Double-check for typos or request a new code on your TV.
  • Website not loading? Try clearing your browser cache or use a different browser.
  • Still stuck? Contact support through the support section on the website.
